⟶ How It Works

Three Simple Steps

Get your book summarized in minutes with no technical knowledge required.

1

Upload Your Book

Upload any PDF or TXT file. Add the book title and author name. Summarium extracts the full text automatically.

2

Choose Your Format

Pick paragraph or bullet format, choose the length — short, medium, or detailed — or generate a visual mind map.

3

Get Your Summary

Summarium processes the book using AI and delivers a clean summary. Copy it, download it as PDF, or regenerate if needed.
